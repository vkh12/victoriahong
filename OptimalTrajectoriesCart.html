Optimal Trajectories for Autonomous Human-Following Carts

Robotics, Automation & Mechatronics Lab at Rutgers University

July 2020 - Present

  • Conduct research on autonomous systems with Dr. Jingang Yi to develop a framework that produces optimal trajectories for contactless carts to achieve safe shopping in crowded environments
  • Utilize RGB cameras for human tracking and activity recognition and Adaptive Monte Carlo Localization (AMCL) to estimate robot poses and calculate optimal trajectories
  • Experiment with ROS/Gazebo simulations using modified Turtlebot3 platforms and in-lab experiments with an omni-directional robot to prove the accuracy and robustness of technology
